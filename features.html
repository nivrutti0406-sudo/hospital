1. CT scan facilites


CT scan is a diagnostic imaging procedure that uses a combination of X-rays and computer technology to produce images of the inside of the body. It shows detailed images of any part of the body, including the bones, muscles, fat, organs and blood vessels









2.clean and comfortable beds


Clean hospital beds are important for patient care, comfort, and safety. They help prevent the spread of infections and other health issues. Why clean hospital beds? Prevent infections: Clean beds help prevent the spread of healthcare-associated infections (HCAIs). Improve patient comfort: Clean beds can make patients more comfortable and dignified. Reduce dust and soiling: Clean beds reduce dust and soiling on surfaces that patients touch less. Prevent functional problems: Regular cleaning prevents a buildup of dirt and oils that can cause functional problems
